About me

Welcome to my world of data-driven innovation!

I am a passionate Data Scientist with over 4 years of hands-on experience crafting cutting-edge AI solutions for enterprise-level challenges. My journey has taken me through diverse domains, from healthcare and cyber-security to the ever-evolving landscape of real estate.

My academic prowess includes a Masters in Intelligent Systems from the esteemed National University of Singapore (NUS), where I immersed myself in the realm of AI, exploring state-of-the-art algorithms and their practical implementation to tackle real-world problems.

Currently, I thrive as a Data Scientist at Real Estate Analytics, where I spearhead the development of next-generation AI solutions that revolutionize the real estate landscape. From conceptualization to deployment and beyond, I am deeply involved in every stage of the project lifecycle, ensuring seamless integration and sustained impact.

I possess a versatile skill set that enables me to seamlessly navigate the complex landscape of data and AI. I excel in retrieving data from diverse databases, including MySQL, Neo4j, and MongoDB, and harness the power of various machine learning frameworks such as scikit-learn, TensorFlow, Keras, and PyTorch to train robust models. Taking it a step further, I can adeptly wrap these models into efficient APIs using web frameworks like Flask and FastAPI, containerize them using Docker and Kubernetes, and effortlessly deploy them to leading cloud platforms like AWS, Azure, and Heroku, ensuring seamless integration.
